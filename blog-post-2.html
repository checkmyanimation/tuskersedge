A slow 12-day Sri Lanka loop

A balanced first-timer route that blends hills, heritage, and sea without rushing.

This loop gives you space to breathe. Two nights in the Cultural Triangle, three in the hill country, and slow coastal days at the end.

Outline

  • Days 1-2: Colombo arrival + transfer to Sigiriya.
  • Days 3-5: Kandy and tea country rail journey.
  • Days 6-9: Ella to south coast for beach time.
  • Days 10-12: Galle, slow mornings, and departure.

We can shorten or extend this depending on your interests, or swap in the east coast during May to September.
